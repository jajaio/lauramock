Revolutionary Teaching

How being aware, not perfect,

is the most powerful tool in the classroom!

At the end of the day, secure, authentic, and compassionate teachers are not only more effective in their academic instruction, but can literally reach students at the soul/emotional level where significant, positive impacts are created.  

Why is this important? Revolutionary Teaching strongly believes that students emotional needs intertwine with their ability to learn and retain academic information. Therefore, supporting the ones providing the educational services to our students is key. For adults, like our students, it is true that long-term performance and success has a direct correlation to our emotional wellbeing. When teachers are connected to their own emotional body, the more effective and powerful they are in the classroom. They are better equipped to support their students emotionally, while providing a balanced educational environment for the highest possible learning!

For students, the benefits are endless! We cannot give what we do not have! Our students are now being encouraged to handle their frustrations in a more authentic, compassionate manner. Just like their teachers, they are encouraged to find their power from within.

How does one do this? This multi-faceted workshop dives into a teachers personal stories, emotions and experiences throughout their life. Through carefully constructed exercises, teachers become aware of how their perception of their past and present experiences continually shape who they are in life and the classroom, both positively and negatively. Fresh perspectives begin to develop, as what may have seemed negative at first, is now seen as powerful gift!

 

Example of full-day or weekend outline:

I.                    Introduction: What is the emotional body and why is it important?

II.                  Setting intentions: Why this changes everything.

III.                Memories: Honoring the past.

IV.               Life is your greatest teacher: How to use experiences from your own life and classroom to become a happier person and a more effective teacher.

Various exercises:

a.      Who do you love?

b.      Developing and honoring your authentic self.

c.      Discerning in the present moment.

d.      What does perfectionism really mean?

e.      Let's talk about that child. The one that keeps you up at night!

V.                 You have all the answers! You always have!

 

Two hour workshops available; outline will be unique depending on the needs and interests of the school. Two hour workshops, 3-4 times a year is recommended.

 

FAQ

·       "How does this program reach everyone?"

Revolutionary teaching naturally differentiates to each participate simply because the participate will decide how deep they can and are ready to go. I strongly believe it is one step at a time. For some educators, these concepts will be brand new. Others will already be familiar with these ideas, and this workshop will help them go even deeper. However, everyone will have the opportunity to take their teaching to another level if they are open and willing to evolve.

·       "Are teachers required to share out loud or with a small group/partner during the workshop?"

No. Just as our students process differently, so do adults! Some of us process better sharing with others, while others need quiet reflection. Some of us are very private while others are an open book...and everything in between. All are perfect, just as they are! Therefore, this program's goal is for everyone to be comfortable and process the way that best suits them.
