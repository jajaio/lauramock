How to embrace conflict:

Disagreements can become a tool instead of creating unwanted drama

 

Unhealthy conflict can be a poison in schools.  Unhealthy conflicts in the classroom deviates from learning. Learn the difference between healthy and destructive conflicts.  This training provides teachers with opportunities to look within, asking questions like, “How can I grow from this?’  instead of, “Why is life out to get me?”.   Looking at conflicts closer through a lens of curiosity and compassion, is a game changer in our classroom and in life!
