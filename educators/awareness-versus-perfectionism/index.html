Awareness versus Perfectionism:

How awareness in the classroom creates immediate, positive results!

 

Mistakes are wonderful opportunities to grow for us all!  It is especially important to provide a safe place for our students to make mistakes. This workshop introduces a new lens in which to interpret mistakes, both for the educator personally, and their students.  For example, when ‘mistakes’ are embraced, students’ maintain a zest for learning as teachers promote these opportunities as healthy challenges.  This in turn, promotes curiosity.  Conversely, mistakes make us feel ‘bad’.  When students feel like they are not good enough, their self-worth drops and so does their enthusiasm to learn.  We want endless opportunities for our students and it begins with the teacher!
